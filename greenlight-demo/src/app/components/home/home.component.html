GreenLight

Streamlining your permit and licensing experience.

GreenLight is a demonstration of the potential of trusted digital credentials, showing how government services might independently automate their permit and licensing application process based on better data, while at the same time simplifying cross-jurisdictional processes for businesses.

This new service is a decentralized workflow application - a mapping tool - that helps you navigate the sometimes tricky license and permit process. It uses cutting-edge blockchain technology to show you not only the steps to getting your permit or license, but more importantly, the interdependencies that might be involved. Using a colour-coded flow diagram, GreenLight helps you build up the credentials you need to get the "green light" to operate!
